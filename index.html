I'm Adnan. I'm a creative guy that loves making new solutions to old things. I love learning new things and I've liked computers, phones and networking ever since I was a child. I'm always up-to-date about their software and hardware. I even like modifying electronics to do new things like replacing the hard drive in iPods with an SD card! I also know a bunch of languages too!

Hobbies

I have a computer that runs both Windows and macOS - it's a hackinntosh. It runs the latest version of macOS great, with graphic acceleration, WiFi and Bluetooth. It works great, however, due to my graphics card being incompatible with macOS, DRM content, like Netflix, doesn't play in Safari, but does work properly in other browsers.
Here are the specs of it if you'd like to build something similar:

  • AMD Ryzen 5 3600
  • Radeon RX 6700 XT
  • 16GB 3200MHz DDR4 RAM in dual channel
  • about 2TB of SSD storage
  • Fenvi T919 WiFi/Bluetooth
  • Windows 11 and macOS Sequoia

I enjoy collecting, repairing, and modifying vintage Apple products, like iPods. I open them, repair them if needed and then modify them with larger batteries, replace the internal spinning hard drive with an SD card and also flash custom firmware on them! It's a really fun hobby and being able to tinker with them is really calming.
