Actuallyari...who?

I'm Adnan, also known as actuallyaridan or simply aridan. I'm a creative guy that loves making new solutions to old things. I love learning new things and I've liked computers, phones and networking ever since I was a child. I'm always up-to-date about their software and hardware. I even like modifying electronics to do new things like replacing the hard drive in iPods with an SD card! I also know a bunch of languages too!

Technology

I have a PC

I've fully built it myself. It runs games quite well and it dual-boots Windows 11 and macOS. Yes, it's indeed a hackintosh! It was a bit tideisous to get it running the first time, but it runs well!

  • AMD Ryzen 5 3600
  • Radeon RX 6700 XT
  • 16GB 3200MHz DDR4 RAM in dual channel
  • about 2TB of SSD storage
  • Fenvi T919 WiFi/Bluetooth
  • Windows 11 and macOS Sequoia

I collect vintage Apple products

I enjoy collecting, repairing, and modifying vintage Apple products, like iPods. I open them, repair them if needed and then modify them with larger batteries, replace the internal spinning hard drive with an SD card and also flash custom firmware on them! It's a really fun hobby and being able to tinker with them is really calming.

I currently have a...

  • a 5.5th Generation iPod Video with a 5000mAh battery and 128GB SD card storage, along with a transparent click wheel and top case
  • a 2nd Generation iPod Mini with a new battery and 64GB of Compact Flash storage
  • a 1st Generation iPod Touch
  • a 3rd Generation iPod Touch

Fun fact!

I have quite bad vision

Nystagmus, two-eyed-monocular vision, hypertropia, hypotropia and myopia.

Yep, that's all the things wrong with my eyes! My eyes like to dance, hate each other, aren't level and can't see clearly far away without glasses.

My brain has learned to deal with it better, and with glasses I can see quite clearly. Crazy how much the brain can adjust itself, eh?
